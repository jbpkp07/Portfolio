About

Welcome! My name is Jeremy Barnes and I am a software engineer based in Los Angeles, CA. While I have over 10 years of experience with systems engineering, I have spent the last 3 years primarily focused on software development. After many nights of coding late into the evening, I realized that not only had I found my calling, but finally the knowledge I gained in earning a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science was being fully applied. Software development has proven to be such a natural fit for my personality, skillset and interests that I’ve decided to transition my career in this direction.

Since discovering my passion for software development, I initially focused my efforts in the C# / .Net ecosystems to write software applications that provide an automated audio/video file encoding pipeline for use at my current company. Eager to learn more, I enrolled in the Full-Stack Software Development Bootcamp through the UCLA Extension to gain competence in additional web stack software technologies. While in the coding bootcamp I have created various front-end, back-end and full-stack projects, as well as RESTful API services.

Nearing the UCLA bootcamp graduation, I find myself armed with a CS degree, experience building a variety of applications using an assortment of programming languages, the assurance in my ability to learn nearly any new language or technology and the motivation to propel my career forward confidently in the direction of software development.
