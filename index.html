About

My name is Jeremy Barnes, and I am a software engineer with 4 years of experience in software development and prior experience in systems engineering.

I work for Spafax — an inflight entertainment company — developing a full stack TypeScript/React/AWS application to automate video encoding and delivery from the cloud, to the seatback screen on your next flight.

I enjoy practicing software craftmanship, learning new coding technologies, developing clever backend algorithms, and delivering a polished frontend experience to share the joy.

The projects in my portfolio were created while attending UCLA to level-up my knowledge of modern web development. I have grown as a developer since and would be thrilled to showcase my current abilities upon request.

Aside from software, I am an avid pro-level disc golfer, NBA/NFL fan, hiker, Mexican food devotee, and loving father.
